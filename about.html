About This Project

SMCC began as MVTI in Augusta in 1946, expanding practical programs to meet post‑war workforce needs.[1][2] By 1952, MVTI opened at Fort Preble in South Portland—an historic coastal defense fort constructed beginning in 1808 and deactivated in 1950.[3][5]

During the 1960s, MVTI became SMVTI with authority to award Associate in Applied Science degrees; in 1989 it became SMTC, added the A.A. in Liberal Studies in 1998, and was renamed SMCC in 2003 with formation of the Maine Community College System.[1]

In 2011, SMCC opened the Midcoast Campus at Brunswick Landing (former Naval Air Station Brunswick), extending access with modern labs and classrooms.[1][10]
